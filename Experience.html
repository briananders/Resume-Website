Multiple Arcade Machine Emulator (MAME) Fall 2011 - Spring 2012

A friend of mine and I had the idea of building an arcade cabinet. The friend is Alex Cash, and during my senior year, we built an arcade cabinet from scratch. We got money to buy all the necessary pieces for construction, like plywood and screws. In my garage, we built the shell for the arcade. We then got a computer and set it up for MAME (Multiple Arcade Machine Emulator). MAME allows one to enjoy all their favorite games from their youth on a modern computer system. Do you like Mario Kart or 007 Golden Eye for the Nintendo 64? With MAME setup correctly and with the correct ROMS, you can play those games again.
